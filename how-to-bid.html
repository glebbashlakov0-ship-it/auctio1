How to Bid

Your complete guide to participating in our auctions

Step 1

Register

Register for free on our platform. Provide your contact information and set up your bidding preferences. Verification is quick and secure.

Step 2

Browse & Research

Explore our current auctions and upcoming lots. Review detailed descriptions, high-resolution images, and condition reports for items of interest.

Step 3

Place Your Bid

Enter your maximum bid amount. Our system will automatically bid on your behalf up to your limit, ensuring you get the best price.

Step 4

Win & Pay

If you win, you'll receive a notification immediately. Payment is due within 7 days. We accept various payment methods for your convenience.

Bidding Tips

Set a Budget

Decide your maximum bid before the auction starts and stick to it. Factor in the buyer's premium and any applicable taxes.

Research Thoroughly

Review the lot details, condition reports, and provenance. Contact our specialists if you have questions about authenticity or condition.

Bid Early

Don't wait until the last minute. Early bids show serious interest and give you time to adjust your strategy if needed.

Auction Types

Live Auctions

Participate in real-time bidding with our auctioneer. Bid online, by phone, or in person at our auction room.

Online Auctions

Browse and bid at your convenience. These auctions run for several days, giving you time to research and decide.

Timed Auctions

All lots close at predetermined times. Similar to online auctions but with specific closing schedules for each lot.
