About Sotheby's

A legacy of excellence in fine art and luxury auctions since 1892

Our Story

Founded in Paris in 1892, Sotheby's has been at the forefront of the international art and luxury goods market for over a century. What began as a small gallery has evolved into one of the world's most prestigious auction houses, known for our expertise, integrity, and exceptional service.

Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us the trusted choice for collectors, institutions, and connoisseurs worldwide. We combine traditional auction house values with cutting-edge technology to provide an unparalleled experience for buyers and sellers alike.
